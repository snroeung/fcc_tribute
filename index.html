About Steve Jobs

  • Co-founded Apple in 1976 with Steve Wozniak. The company's first product was called the Apple I, a computer designed and built by Wozniak
  • Was the chairman and chief executive officer (CEO) of Apple until his death in 2011. He was also the chairman and major stakeholder of Pixar and founded NeXT
  • Recognized as a prioner of the personal computer revolution of the 1970s and 1980s, alongside Steve Wozinak
